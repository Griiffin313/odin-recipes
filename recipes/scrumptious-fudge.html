Scrumptious Fudge Recipe

Description

For the sweet tooths out there looking for a snack on a lazy Sunday afternoon, fudge is ALWAYS a good option.

Ingredient list

  • 1 Can (397g) Sweetened condensed milk
  • 150ml Milk
  • 450g Demerara Sugar
  • 115g Butter

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a large non-stick saucepan.
  2. Melt it over low heat, stirring non-stop until the sugar has dissolved
  3. Raise the temperature to a steady boil for 10-15 mins and keep on stirring. Scrape the base of the pan to ensure it doesn't burn
  4. Remove from heat and let cool for a few minutes
  5. Beat the mixture for 5-10 minutes until it loses its glossiness and thickens. Stir for longer if you want crumbly fudge
  6. Press into a flat pan on baking parchment to cool. Cut into cubes when cool.
