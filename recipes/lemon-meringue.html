Easy Lemon Meringue Recipe

Description

Want a quick Lemon Meringue recipe that will always leave your guests in awe? I have tried various recipes and always end up coming back to this one!

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 ½ cups of graham crackers (tennis biscuits)
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter (melted)

Filling

  • 4 large egg yolks (use teh egg whites for the meringue
  • 1 can (375 ml) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest
  • ½ cup fresh lemon juice (should take about 3 lemons)

Meringue

  • 4 large egg whites
  • 6 tablespoons sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon cream of tartar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190 °C and adjust the oven rack to the middle of the oven.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mix together the graham cracker crumbs and sugar.
  3. Add the melted butter and stir with a fork first. Next stir with your hadns and ensure the mixture is well combined.
  4. Add the mixture to a 23cm pie pan and press it down firmly with the bottom of a glass or mug until the crust is about ½ cm thick.
  5. Bake for about 10 minutes or until slightly browned.
  6. Whisk egg yolks, lemon zest, lemon juice and condensed milk in a medium mixing bowl.
  7. Pour the wet mixture into the crust.
  8. Whisk (medium to high speed) egg whites in a stand mixer and slowly add the sugar.
  9. Add the cream of tartar when all sugar has been added and dissolved.
  10. Stop mixing only when stiff and shiny peaks form.
  11. Reduce oven temperature to 160°C.
  12. Using a spatula or big spoon, spread the meringue over the pie filling. Cover all sides of the crust and decorate by pulling the meringue up into peaks with the spoon or spatula.
  13. Bake for about 20 minutes or until lightly browned.
  14. (optional) Use a blowtorch to brown the edges of the meringue peaks. Careful not to overdo it.
  15. Let the pie cool completely over a few hours to allow the filling to set.
